About Us

"Impulse 2018" is the Third edition of the annual technical Fest of the Department of Electrical Engineering Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur organised by the Electrical Engineering Society. We aim at bringing the students of Electrical/Electronics Engineering from various colleges to develop new breakthroughs in their respective fields that later might bring a revolution in the field. If you have an idea we are providing you a platform to showcase your talent in front of the best in the country. The event spans across an entire day of intense contests, challenging problems and spectacular presentations here in Kharagpur. If you have got what it takes to stand out among the rest, we welcome you here at "Impulse".

Event FAQs

Who Can Participate and how to register for Impulse?

Impulse 2018 is open to all. You can register on the following link: Click here

How do I reach IIT Kharagpur ?

The Kharagpur(KGP) station is connected to all major cities of the country by train.If your city does not have a direct rail route to Kharagpur, then catch a train to Kolkata. Kharagpur is situated 120 Kms off Kolkata which is well connected to all the cities of India by rail. Trains from Kolkata to Kharagpur are available round the clock.The IIT Campus is just 5 kms away from KGP station and is accessible round the clock via cabs, rickshaws and autos.

What are the events featured in Impulse 2018 ?

There are 8 events to be conducted in IMPULSE 2018. These are :
  • Digianatronix
  • Data Analytics
  • Case Study
  • Enigma
  • Embedit
  • Re-Energize
  • Quizmania
  • Treasure Hunt

How many events can I participate in ? Do I need to register for each event individually ?

You can participate in as many events you wish to. There is no restriction. You need to register for the events individually as and when the event registration portal opens. If you are unable to form a team with required number of participants, we can pair you with other students. There is no restriction regarding participants forming teams from different colleges. Students need to carry their Institute ID cards or Equivalent document issued by their institute.

What are the particulars for registration and accomodation ?

Accommodation will be provided to those who are interested. Registration for IMPULSE is free. You need to pay a fees of Rs. 200 for accommodation. If you don't want accomodation, you need not pay anything. If you are unable to participate due to some unforeseen reasons, You have to inform us before 18th March. No Refund will be provided after that. The registration for the following events can also be done on-the-spot : Digianatronix, Enigma, Treasure Hunt and Quizmania. However we insist all that all participants should complete their registration before the last date given on the Website of IMPULSE. For all other events registration has to be done before their respective deadlines .

Any details about the food, security and health at campus ?

IIT Kharagpur has some delicious food waiting for you! Check out here. We have round the clock security everywhere in the campus. Security guards are always on duty at the security counter of all the hostels. Volunteers will also be present at the accommodation venues. But still we request you to avoid carrying expensive items. IMPULSE team does not bear any responsibility for any loss of personal belongings. We have a medical hospital - the BC ROY Hospital inside the campus that will take care of your medical problems, if any. In case of any illness, you or your team members can contact the control tent and we shall ensure immediate action.

What are the prizes to be won and when do I receive it ? Are there any certificate of participation ?

Complete details of all the events are provided in individual event portals on the official website of IMPULSE. All the results will be announced on the second day , i.e. 25th March in the award ceremony. Participants of all the events will get certificate of participation.

When will be the problem statements of the events released ?

Problem statements for the events will be duly released on the IMPULSE website. Please checkout here.
